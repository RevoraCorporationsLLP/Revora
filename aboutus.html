About Us

With core values of quality and a philosophy that spells out “You Imagine, We Deliver”, Revora is largely customer centric, serving only the best when it comes to bathroom fittings and infrastructures. The company is a leading innovator in the way one uses water everyday- after all, the best Eureka moments do happen in the shower! With a consumer base spanning India, Bangladesh, Bhutan, Nepal, Thailand and Indonesia, the company is well known owing to its advanced technology further strengthened by great after sales services offered.

Revora’s products find usage in a wide array of high end homes, hotels, public and private offices and the likes. With its presence and strong hold in about 51 cities, the company aims to break into the international market with its new range of products.
